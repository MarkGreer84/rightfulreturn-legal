Article I ‚Äî Formation and Purpose

This Agreement establishes a General Partnership between the following two private trusts:

  • Greer Dynasty Revocable Living Trust (GDRLT)
  • GD Business Holdings Trust (GDBHT)

Collectively referred to as the General Partnership / Texas Revocable Living Trust (GPTRL). The GPTRL is formed as a private, unincorporated business entity for the purposes of administering private contract enforcement, serving as an arbitration board for affiliated PMAs and private trusts, and managing neutral governance between estate and business domains.

Article II ‚Äî Jurisdiction and Capacity

The GPTRL operates exclusively under the private domain and shall not be registered with any public agency, shall not apply for an EIN, and shall not engage in regulated public commerce. Jurisdiction is maintained solely by private contract.

Article III ‚Äî Trustees as Partners

Each Trust shall act through its designated Trustee(s), who shall represent the Trust in all GPTRL matters, vote on arbitration issues, and may appoint a Private Overseer if needed. No unilateral action is permitted.

Article IV ‚Äî Arbitration Authority

The GPTRL shall serve as the binding arbitration body for all disputes involving PMA members, affiliated trusts, or business entities under contract. Remedies may include private liens, revocations, expulsions, or other lawful outcomes.

Article V ‚Äî Liability Limitation

Liability is limited strictly to the GPTRL-related interests of the participating trusts. Trustees, beneficiaries, and outside trust assets are not exposed to risk or judgment.

Article VI ‚Äî Term Length and Renewal

This Agreement remains in effect for 50 years, renewable in 25-year terms by mutual reaffirmation of both Trustees. Early dissolution requires full consent and recordkeeping of all decisions.

Article VII ‚Äî Indemnification and Protections

All Trustees are indemnified against legal or private actions for acts taken in good faith under this Agreement, with exceptions for gross negligence or misconduct.

Article VIII ‚Äî Confidentiality and Recordkeeping

All records are to be securely stored and never disclosed publicly. GPTRL actions are exempt from public records laws and may be encrypted and archived privately.

Article IX ‚Äî Amendments and Modifications

Changes may be made only by written mutual agreement of both Trustees, logged and stored in the same private and secure manner as this document.

Article X ‚Äî Dissolution

Dissolution must be mutually agreed and documented, with assets, records, and rulings returned to the original Trusts' jurisdiction per their own succession plans.
