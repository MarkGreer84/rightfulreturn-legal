⚖️ This Is Education, Not Piracy

Rightful Return PMA references statutes, rulings, news, books, and public records. But we don’t steal. We cite it, honor it, and offer commentary, criticism, or context — as protected under:

  • 17 U.S.C. § 107 – U.S. Fair Use Law
  • Common Law & PMA Private Use Protections

🧠 What We Do With Quoted Content

  • Use short excerpts or relevant sections for teaching and discussion
  • Add our own editorial commentary or criticism
  • Credit authors fully, even when quoting small pieces
  • Sometimes recommend full books or articles
  • Never alter original meaning

🚫 What We Do Not Do

  • We don’t monetize quoted material
  • We don’t claim other people’s work as our own
  • We don’t rewrite someone else’s message to say something it didn’t

📌 Scope of Application

This disclaimer applies to all domains, subdomains, materials, and member-facing communications associated with Rightful Return PMA.

🔐 Final Note

This site operates under the protections of private contract and lawful educational freedom. Our intention is never to misuse — only to honor and expand thought. If quoting truth is now controversial, so be it. We'll keep quoting.
