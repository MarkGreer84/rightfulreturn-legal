🧠 Clarifying Our Mission and Common Questions

1  🐾 Common Misunderstandings — Let’s Clear Them Up

  • “Is this a tax shelter?” – No. Rightful Return is not, and never has been, structured for tax avoidance or evasion.
  • “Is this a scam?” – Absolutely not. Rightful Return is a lawful, educational PMA with a member-signed contract and 48-hour review period offered.
  • “Is this legal advice?” – No. Rightful Return does not offer legal advice, nor act as a legal service provider.
  • “Is this a get-rich-quick scheme?” – No. We teach a slow, document-based recovery process rooted in due diligence.
